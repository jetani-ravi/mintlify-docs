<Update label="Latest" description="Documentation launch">
  ## Help center launched

  The Autocampaign AI help center is live, covering:

  - **Channels & providers** — SMS (Twilio, Telnyx) and email (verified domains and
    senders).
  - **Compliance** — A2P 10DLC, opt-in, and opt-out.
  - **Contacts** — import, custom fields, tags, and dynamic segments.
  - **Campaigns** — broadcasts, templates, polls, and links.
  - **Automation & AI** — workflow builder, AI agent, and knowledge base.
  - **Inbox, Scheduling, Advertising, Analytics, Billing, and Developer** tools.

  Have feedback on the docs? Email [support@autocampaign.ai](mailto:support@autocampaign.ai).
</Update>
