## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I send my first message?">
    Connect an SMS provider ([Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Telnyx](/providers/telnyx)),
    [import contacts](/contacts/import-csv), then create a [campaign](/campaigns/overview).
    See the [Quickstart](/quickstart).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need my own Twilio/Telnyx account?">
    Yes — you connect your own provider account. EzyText sends through it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I invite my team?">
    From **Settings → Users → Invite member**. See [Invite your team](/getting-started/invite-team).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Compliance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to register 10DLC?">
    For reliable delivery to US mobile numbers, yes. See [A2P 10DLC](/compliance/a2p-10dlc).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are opt-outs handled?">
    STOP and similar keywords opt a contact out automatically, and they're excluded
    from future campaigns. See [Opt-out management](/compliance/opt-out).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the opt-in auto-reply written by AI?">
    No — it's a static message you configure. AI replies are a separate
    [AI agent](/automation/ai-agent) feature for the inbox.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Contacts & campaigns

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use custom fields as merge tags?">
    No — merge tags are limited to standard contact fields. See
    [Custom fields](/contacts/custom-fields).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a campaign target multiple segments?">
    One segment per campaign today. Build a dynamic segment that captures everyone you
    want. See [Segments](/contacts/segments).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do replies show up?">
    In the unified [Inbox](/inbox/overview), linked to the contact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I export contacts?">
    Contact CSV export isn't available today; import and in-app segmentation are.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Email

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect Gmail or Outlook to send email?">
    No — EzyText sends from your **verified domain**. There's no mailbox or SMTP
    connection. See [Email channel](/channels/email).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Automation & AI

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can the AI agent book meetings or update a CRM?">
    Not today. Its abilities are knowledge-base search and human handover. See
    [AI agent](/automation/ai-agent).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a 'contact replied' workflow trigger?">
    Use a **Wait → until something happens → Contact replies** step inside a
    [workflow](/automation/workflow-builder).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is billing a prepaid SMS wallet?">
    It's a subscription with entitlements and add-ons; **AI credits** are the
    wallet-like balance. See [Plans & billing](/billing/wallet-billing).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
