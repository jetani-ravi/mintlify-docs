Common issues and fixes, grouped by area. If you're still stuck, contact
[support@autocampaign.ai](mailto:support@autocampaign.ai).

## SMS provider connection

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Twilio: 'Invalid credentials'">
    Re-copy your **Account SID** (`AC…`) and **Auth Token** (32 chars) from the Twilio
    Console → Account Info. Watch for trailing spaces and rotated tokens. See
    [Connect Twilio](/providers/twilio).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Telnyx: 'API key is missing required permissions'">
    Your API key needs the **Messaging** scope. Recreate the key with Messaging
    enabled. See [Connect Telnyx](/providers/telnyx).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Replies aren't arriving">
    Finish the setup wizard so webhooks are registered, and confirm the number is
    attached to a managed messaging service/profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## SMS delivery (common Twilio error codes)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="30007 — message filtered / blocked by carrier">
    Usually a content or compliance issue. Register [10DLC](/compliance/a2p-10dlc),
    avoid spam-trigger wording and shortened public URLs, and keep opt-in/opt-out
    language clear.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="30034 — unregistered / pending 10DLC">
    The sending number isn't associated with an approved A2P campaign. Complete
    [10DLC registration](/compliance/a2p-10dlc) and attach the number to the
    registered messaging service.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="21608 — number not enabled / trial restriction">
    The from-number can't send to that destination (e.g. a trial number to an
    unverified recipient). Verify the number and destination, or upgrade the provider
    account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="21610 — recipient has opted out (STOP)">
    Expected when messaging an opted-out contact — the carrier blocks it. Autocampaign AI
    already excludes opted-out contacts; see [Opt-out](/compliance/opt-out).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## A2P 10DLC

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Brand rejected / Unverified">
    Almost always an identity mismatch. Ensure your **Legal Business Name**, **EIN**,
    and **address** exactly match your IRS records, then resubmit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Brand is not approved yet' when creating a campaign">
    Wait until the brand badge is green (Approved/Verified) before registering a
    campaign.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign rejected (TCR/MNO Rejected)">
    Tighten your opt-in flow description and make sample messages match your use case.
    See [A2P 10DLC](/compliance/a2p-10dlc#troubleshooting).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Email

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Domain stuck on 'Pending'">
    Check each CNAME host/value matches exactly (your DNS provider may auto-append the
    domain). Propagation can take up to 48 hours. See [Sending domains](/providers/email-domains).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Domain not verified' when sending">
    You can only send from a **Verified** domain and a sender on it. Complete DNS
    verification first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Email must end with @yourdomain'">
    A sender's address must belong to its verified domain. See
    [Senders](/providers/email-senders).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Advertising

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Permission wasn't granted' / OAuth failed">
    You declined a permission during Google/Facebook sign-in. Reconnect and allow
    access. See [Google Ads](/providers/google-ads) / [Meta Ads](/providers/meta-ads).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'No ad accounts found'">
    You signed in with an account that has no ad accounts. Reconnect with the account
    that manages your ads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Account shows 'Needs attention'">
    Access expired or was revoked. Click **Reconnect** to restore lead flow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Webhooks

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Signature verification fails">
    Sign `<timestamp>.<raw-body>` (raw bytes, pre-parse) with HMAC-SHA256 using your
    signing secret, and compare to `v1` in `X-Autocampaign-Signature`. See
    [Webhooks](/developers/webhooks).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My endpoint was auto-disabled">
    Repeated failures disable an endpoint. Fix it and re-activate to reset the failure
    streak.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Contacts & import

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="CSV rows failed with 'Invalid phone number format'">
    Phone values allow digits, spaces, hyphens, parentheses, and a leading `+`, up to
    20 characters. Clean the column and re-import. See [Import](/contacts/import-csv).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'File does not appear to contain valid CSV text'">
    Re-export as **CSV UTF-8** (not `.xlsx`) and keep it under 10 MB / 50,000 rows.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
