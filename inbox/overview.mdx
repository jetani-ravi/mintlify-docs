## Overview

The inbox is where you have conversations with contacts. Every inbound reply lands
here, linked to the contact, so you (and your team, and your
[AI agent](/automation/ai-agent)) can respond from one place. Open it from
**Inbox** in the sidebar (`/inbox`).

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner
</Info>

## Layout

The inbox has three panels:

- **Left** — the conversation list, with search, filters, sort, and a **Pinned**
  section.
- **Middle** — the conversation thread and message composer.
- **Right** — the contact's details, tags, opt-out status, notes, and the **AI
  assistant** control.

Tabs above the list filter by **All**, **Unread**, **Read**, and **Archived**.

## Work a conversation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a conversation">
    Click a conversation. It's marked read automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reply">
    Type in the composer and send (Enter to send, Shift+Enter for a new line). Insert
    a [quick message](/campaigns/quick-messages) by typing `/`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an internal note">
    Switch to **note** mode to leave a private note for your team — not visible to the
    contact. Notes support attachments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign, pin, or archive">
    Use the thread header to **assign** the conversation to a teammate, **pin** it, or
    **archive** it when you're done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Channels

Conversations can span **SMS**, **WhatsApp**, and **email** (shown as channel tabs
in the thread). SMS shows a character/segment counter. Sending is blocked when the
contact is opted out, the conversation is archived, or there's no connected number.

<Note>
  The email composer is read-only for now — email sending from the inbox is coming
  in a later release.
</Note>

## Schedule a message

Use the send button's dropdown to **schedule** a message for later. Scheduled
messages appear in a panel where you can reschedule, send now, or cancel them.

## AI assistant

In the contact panel, the **AI assistant** card lets you **assign** an
[AI agent](/automation/ai-agent) to handle the conversation, **take over** to reply
yourself, or **resume AI**. AI-authored replies are labeled with the agent's name.

## Real-time

The inbox updates live — new messages, status changes, assignments, and notes
appear instantly for your whole team without refreshing.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I 'close' a conversation?">
    Use **Archive** — it moves the conversation to the Archived tab and stops replies.
    Reopen it anytime.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I filter to just my conversations?">
    Yes — open **Filters** and set **Assigned to → Me**. You can also filter by
    channel, tags, and date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I send an email reply?">
    Email replies from the inbox aren't enabled yet. Email campaigns still send
    normally.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do quick messages support variables?">
    No — quick messages are plain text. See [Quick messages](/campaigns/quick-messages).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't send a reply">
    Check that the contact isn't opted out, the conversation isn't archived, and you
    have a connected number for the channel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The AI stopped replying">
    Sending a manual reply pauses the AI (Human controlled). Use **Resume AI** to hand
    it back. See [AI agent](/automation/ai-agent).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
