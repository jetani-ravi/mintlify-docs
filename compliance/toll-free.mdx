## Overview

Toll-free numbers (numbers starting with 800, 888, 877, etc.) are an alternative to
10-digit local numbers for A2P messaging. Like local numbers, they must be
**verified** with the carriers before you can send at scale — otherwise your
messages are heavily rate-limited or blocked.

<Note>
  For standard 10-digit local numbers, use [A2P 10DLC registration](/compliance/a2p-10dlc)
  instead. This page is about **toll-free** numbers specifically.
</Note>

## Buying a toll-free number

You can purchase a toll-free number from within Autocampaign AI: on your SMS channel's
**Numbers** tab, click **Buy New Number**, choose your country, and filter by the
**toll-free** number type before searching.

## Toll-free verification

Toll-free A2P verification is submitted through your connected provider
([Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Telnyx](/providers/telnyx)). The carriers review
your business, use case, opt-in method, and sample messages — similar in spirit to
10DLC, but for toll-free traffic.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy or identify your toll-free number">
    Purchase one in Autocampaign AI or use an existing toll-free number on your provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit toll-free verification with your provider">
    Complete the toll-free verification form in your provider's console with your
    business details, opt-in flow, and sample messages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    Approval typically takes several business days. Until then, keep volume low.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Toll-free or 10DLC — which should I use?">
    10DLC uses standard local numbers and suits most senders. Toll-free is useful for
    a recognizable national number and can offer high throughput once verified. You
    can use either; the registration paths differ.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I still need consent and opt-out handling?">
    Yes. All the same compliance rules apply — collect consent and honor
    [opt-outs](/compliance/opt-out) regardless of number type.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
    Typically several business days, depending on carrier review.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Toll-free messages are being filtered">
    An unverified toll-free number is heavily throttled. Complete toll-free
    verification with your provider, and make sure your opt-in flow and sample
    messages are consistent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
