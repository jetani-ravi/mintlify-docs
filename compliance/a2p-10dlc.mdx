## What is A2P 10DLC?

**A2P 10DLC** (Application-to-Person, 10-Digit Long Code) is the US carrier
framework for sending business messages from standard 10-digit numbers. Carriers
require every business (a **brand**) and every messaging use case (a **campaign**)
to be registered with The Campaign Registry (TCR). Registered senders get higher
throughput and reliable delivery; unregistered traffic is filtered or blocked.

Autocampaign AI handles registration for both [Twilio](/providers/twilio) and
[Telnyx](/providers/telnyx) from inside the app — you fill in two forms and Autocampaign AI
submits them to the carriers on your behalf.

<Note>
  10DLC applies to **US mobile numbers**. If you only message outside the US, you
  may not need it — but registering is still recommended for deliverability.
</Note>

## The registration sequence

Registration has a strict order because each step depends on the previous one:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your provider">
    Connect [Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Telnyx](/providers/telnyx) first —
    brand and campaign registration run through your connected account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your Brand">
    Submit your business details. The brand must be **approved** before you can
    register a campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Messaging Service / Profile">
    A campaign must be linked to a messaging service (Twilio) or messaging profile
    (Telnyx).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your Campaign">
    Submit your use case, sample messages, and opt-in details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach phone numbers">
    Assign numbers to the messaging service so they send under the approved campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You **cannot** register a campaign until the brand shows an approved status
  (`APPROVED`, `VERIFIED`, or `ACTIVE`). Attempting earlier returns: *"Brand is not
  approved yet (current status: …). Wait for approval before registering the campaign."*
</Warning>

## Register your Brand

Go to your SMS channel's **Brand & Campaign** tab and click **Register 10DLC
Brand** (also available as the first step of the setup wizard). The **Create Brand**
drawer has three sections.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Business Information" icon="building">
    | Field | Required | Notes |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | **Brand Type** | Optional | Standard or Sole Proprietor |
    | **Legal Business Name** | Required | Your registered legal name |
    | **Business Type** | Required | Private / Public Company, Non-Profit, Government, Partnership |
    | **EIN (Tax ID)** | Required | e.g. `12-3456789` |
    | **Business Industry** | Required | e.g. Retail, Healthcare, Technology (24 options) |
    | **Company Type** | Required | Private / Public / Non-Profit / Government |
    | **Website URL** | Required | Must be a valid `https://` URL |
    | **Brand Contact Email** | Required | Used for 2FA verification |
    | **Social Media URLs** | Optional | LinkedIn, X, etc. |
    | **Stock Exchange / Ticker** | Required for **public** companies only | Shown only when Company Type = Public |
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Authorized Representative" icon="user">
    | Field | Required | Notes |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | **First / Last Name** | Required | The authorized contact |
    | **Email Address** | Required | Valid email |
    | **Phone Number** | Required | E.164 format, e.g. `+14155552671` |
    | **Job Title** | Required | e.g. CEO |
    | **Job Position** | Required | Director, GM, VP, CEO, CFO, CTO, COO, Other |
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Physical Business Address" icon="location-dot">
    | Field | Required | Notes |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | **Street Address (Line 1)** | Required | |
    | **Street Address (Line 2)** | Optional | |
    | **City / State / ZIP** | Required | ZIP allows letters, digits, spaces, hyphens |
    | **Country** | Required | 2-letter ISO code, e.g. `US` |
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Click **Create Brand**. You'll see *"10DLC brand submitted for carrier
registration."* The brand starts as **Pending** and updates automatically as the
carrier reviews it.

<Tip>
  Your **EIN and legal business name must exactly match** your IRS records. A
  mismatch is the most common cause of brand rejection.
</Tip>

## Register your Campaign

Once your brand is approved, open the **Brand & Campaign** tab and click
**Create new A2P Campaign**. Fill in:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Brand & use case">
    - **A2P Brand** — your approved brand.
    - **Use case** — pick from 15 options with monthly pricing, e.g. **Marketing**
      ($10/mo), **Mixed** ($10), **2FA** ($10), **Account Notification** ($10),
      **Customer Care** ($10), **Low Volume Mixed** ($2), **Sole Proprietor** ($2).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Messaging service">
    Either **create a new messaging service/profile** (enter a name) or **select an
    existing** one. The campaign is linked to it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign description & opt-in flow">
    - **Campaign description** — minimum **40 characters**. Describe what the
      campaign is used for.
    - **Opt-in flow** — minimum **40 characters** (max 2048). Describe exactly how
      people consent to receive messages. **Required by carriers.**
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sample messages">
    - **Sample message #1 and #2** — required, minimum **20 characters** each.
    - **Sample #3–#5** — optional. Provide realistic examples of what you'll send.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Message content flags">
    Check any that apply: embedded links, phone numbers, direct lending / loan
    content, or age-gated content. Carriers use these to assess your campaign.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Auto-reply messages (optional)">
    Optional **opt-in confirmation**, **opt-out confirmation**, and **help** replies
    (20–320 characters each). If you leave keywords unset, Autocampaign AI applies standard
    defaults: opt-in `START, YES`; opt-out `STOP, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE`;
    help `HELP, INFO`.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Click **Create**. You'll see *"10DLC campaign submitted for carrier
registration."* The campaign starts as **In progress**.

<Warning>
  Your **sample messages and opt-in flow must be consistent** with each other and
  with your website. Carriers reject campaigns whose samples don't match the stated
  use case or whose opt-in description is vague.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does approval take?">
    Brand verification is often quick; campaign approval typically takes **1–2
    business days**. Some use cases (like political or charity) may take longer due
    to additional carrier vetting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between a brand and a campaign?">
    A **brand** is your business identity (registered once). A **campaign** is a
    specific messaging use case (marketing, 2FA, notifications). One brand can have
    multiple campaigns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an EIN? What if I'm a sole proprietor?">
    Standard brands require an EIN. Sole proprietors can register using the **Sole
    Proprietor** brand type / use case ($2/mo), which has lower throughput limits
    and a lighter verification path.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit my sample messages after approval?">
    Changing a campaign's use case or samples generally requires re-review by the
    carrier. Register the campaign that matches how you'll actually message.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do daily limits depend on my brand?">
    Yes. Your daily message throughput is determined by your **brand trust score**,
    which the carriers assign during vetting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are opt-out keywords included automatically?">
    Yes. If you don't set custom keywords, Autocampaign AI applies standard opt-out
    (`STOP, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE`), opt-in (`START, YES`), and help
    (`HELP, INFO`) keywords. See [Opt-out management](/compliance/opt-out).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Brand rejected or 'Unverified'">
    Almost always an identity-data mismatch. Verify that your **Legal Business
    Name**, **EIN**, and **address** exactly match your IRS/official records, then
    edit and resubmit. A `.gov`/`.org` mismatch for non-profits is also common.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Brand is not approved yet' when creating a campaign">
    The brand badge isn't green yet. Wait until it shows **Approved / Verified**
    before registering a campaign — the campaign form is gated on brand approval.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign rejected (TCR Rejected / MNO Rejected)">
    Common causes: opt-in flow too vague, sample messages that don't match the use
    case, missing STOP/HELP handling in samples, or links to a domain that doesn't
    describe the messaging program. Tighten your opt-in description and samples,
    then resubmit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Customer Profile is not compliant' (Twilio)">
    Twilio's underlying TrustHub business profile isn't approved. Complete Twilio's
    business profile verification in the Twilio Console, then retry brand
    registration in Autocampaign AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't delete a brand">
    *"Cannot delete brand: N campaign(s) still connected. Delete the campaigns
    first."* Remove the dependent campaigns before deleting the brand.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
