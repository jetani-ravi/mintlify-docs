## Overview

Opt-in is how a contact who previously opted out can **re-subscribe** to your
messages. When someone replies with an opt-in keyword like **START**, EzyText
clears their opt-out flag, records the consent on their timeline, and can send an
optional welcome-back confirmation.

You configure opt-in keywords and the auto-reply at **Settings → Opt-out**, in the
right-hand **Opt-In Settings** column (next to [Opt-out](/compliance/opt-out)).

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Permission:** viewing needs Settings
  read; changes need Settings update (Owner/Admin).
</Info>

## How opt-in works

<Steps>
  <Step title="A contact replies with an opt-in keyword">
    EzyText checks inbound messages for opt-in keywords. Detection is the same as
    opt-out: case-insensitive, start-anchored, whole-word (`STARTUP` won't match
    `START`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="The contact is re-subscribed">
    If the contact was previously opted out, EzyText clears the flag, records an
    **Opted in** event on their timeline, and they become eligible for campaigns
    again. If they were never opted out, nothing changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="An optional confirmation is sent">
    If you've set an opt-in auto-reply, EzyText sends it (subject to the
    provider-reserved keyword rules below).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Default keywords

Every workspace starts with the carrier-standard opt-in keywords:

```
START, UNSTOP, YES
```

<Note>
  Opt-in only affects contacts who are **currently opted out**. An opt-in keyword
  from an unknown number (one that has never contacted you) is ignored — it does
  not create a contact.
</Note>

## Configure opt-in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open opt-in settings">
    Go to **Settings → Opt-out** and use the **Opt-In Settings** column on the right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage keywords">
    Add custom re-subscribe keywords (for example, `subscribe`) under **Keywords**.
    Up to **75** per workspace, same rules as opt-out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a welcome-back auto-reply (optional)">
    Under **Auto-Reply Message**, enter an optional confirmation (up to **1,600
    characters**) sent when a contact re-subscribes, then **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## About "auto-reply" and AI

The opt-in and opt-out auto-replies are **static messages you configure** — the
exact text you enter is sent back verbatim. They are **not** AI-generated.

If you want AI to answer, qualify, or route **inbound conversations**, that's a
separate feature — see the [AI agent](/automation/ai-agent), which works alongside
your [Inbox](/inbox/overview). Compliance replies (opt-in/opt-out confirmations)
remain deterministic on purpose.

## Provider-reserved keywords

Like opt-out, providers reserve the standard opt-in keywords:

- **Twilio** automatically handles `START, UNSTOP, YES` and sends its own
  confirmation, so EzyText skips its reply for those. Your custom keywords are
  answered by EzyText.
- **Telnyx** behaves similarly when advanced opt-in sync is applied on the profile.

## Consent and audit trail

EzyText records how each contact entered your list via a **consent source**, such
as CSV import, manual entry, web form ([join link](/campaigns/join-links)), text
link, inbound SMS, or an ad platform (Google/Meta). Join-link web forms also store
a snapshot of the exact consent wording, timestamp, and other evidence per
submission. Opt-in and opt-out events are logged on the contact's activity
timeline for compliance.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does replying START always re-subscribe someone?">
    Only if they were opted out. If they were never opted out, the keyword is a
    no-op — no change and no reply.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will an opt-in from a brand-new number create a contact?">
    No. Unlike opt-out (which records STOP even from unknown numbers for
    compliance), opt-in from a number that never contacted you is ignored.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the opt-in confirmation written by AI?">
    No. It's the static message you configure. For AI-assisted inbound replies, use
    the [AI agent](/automation/ai-agent).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why didn't my welcome-back message send?">
    For provider-reserved keywords (START, UNSTOP, YES) the carrier sends its own
    reply and blocks EzyText's. Custom keywords you add are answered by EzyText.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A re-subscribed contact still isn't getting campaigns">
    Confirm an **Opted in** event appears on their timeline. If they opted in after
    a campaign's audience was built, they'll be included in the next send.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom opt-in keyword isn't working">
    Check it's spelled correctly and that the inbound message *starts* with it.
    Matching is start-anchored and whole-word.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
