## Overview

Connecting Meta Ads lets EzyText capture **Lead Ads** from Facebook and Instagram
as contacts automatically. You connect by signing in with Facebook — no app secret
or token to enter.

## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Go to **Advertising → Meta Ads** and click **Connect Meta Ads**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Facebook">
    Click **Continue with Facebook** and grant access to your ads and leads. EzyText
    never changes your ads or spend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an ad account">
    Pick which **ad account** should send you leads.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    Review your campaigns and click **Done**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After connecting

- **Lead Ads** submissions arrive as contacts with source **Meta Ads**, matched by
  phone to existing contacts.
- Trigger a welcome [workflow](/automation/workflow-builder) on **New advertising
  lead**.
- Measure results on **Campaign Performance** and [Ad attribution](/analytics/ad-attribution).

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need Meta Business verification?">
    You connect via Facebook login. If your ad account requires business
    verification to access lead data, complete that in Meta Business settings first.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does it capture both Facebook and Instagram leads?">
    Yes — Lead Ads from both surface as contacts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I enter an app secret?">
    No. Connection is via Facebook sign-in; credentials are managed by EzyText.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Permission wasn't granted'">
    You declined a permission during Facebook login. Reconnect and allow ads and
    leads access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Leads aren't arriving">
    Confirm the correct ad account is selected and the connection shows **Receiving
    leads**. Reconnect if it says **Needs attention**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'No ad accounts found'">
    The Facebook login has no accessible ad accounts. Sign in with the account that
    manages your ads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
