## Overview

To send email from your own address, you verify the domain it belongs to by adding
a few DNS records. This authenticates your mail (SPF and DKIM) and sets up link
tracking, so your campaigns are trusted and land in inboxes. Manage domains at
**Settings → Email → Sending domains** (`/settings/email/domains`).

## Verify a domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your domain">
    Go to **Settings → Sending domains** and click **Add domain**. Enter a bare
    domain like `acme.com` (no `https://`, no path).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the DNS records">
    Autocampaign AI shows a set of **CNAME** records covering **SPF**, **DKIM**, and a
    **tracking domain**. Copy each record's host and value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add them at your DNS provider">
    Open your DNS provider (GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap, etc.) and add the records
    exactly as shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Back in Autocampaign AI, click **Verify**. Autocampaign AI re-checks your DNS and updates the
    status. It also re-checks automatically every few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verification status

- **Pending** — waiting for DNS to propagate (can take minutes up to 48 hours).
- **Verified** — authenticated and ready to send from.
- **Failed** — DNS wasn't detected within 48 hours; fix the records and re-verify.

<Note>
  DNS changes can take time to propagate. Autocampaign AI re-checks pending domains
  automatically — you don't have to keep clicking **Verify**.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What records do I add?">
    CNAME records for SPF, DKIM, and a tracking subdomain — Autocampaign AI generates them
    for your domain. A DMARC record isn't part of this flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to own the domain?">
    Yes — you must be able to add DNS records for it. You can't verify a domain you
    don't control (like `gmail.com`).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
    Often a few minutes, but DNS can take up to 48 hours to propagate globally.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Enter a bare domain like acme.com'">
    Remove the scheme and path — enter just `acme.com`, not `https://acme.com/`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Domain stuck on 'Pending'">
    Double-check each CNAME host and value matches exactly (watch for your provider
    auto-appending the domain). Propagation can take up to 48 hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'This domain is already registered'">
    The domain is already added to your workspace. Open it from the list instead of
    adding it again.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
