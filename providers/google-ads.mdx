## Overview

Connecting Google Ads lets EzyText watch your account for new lead-form leads and
add them to your contacts automatically. You connect by signing in with Google —
there's no developer token or API key to enter.

## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Go to **Advertising → Google Ads** and click **Connect Google Ads** (or start
    from **Connected Accounts → Connect**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    Click **Continue with Google**. You'll be sent to Google to sign in and grant
    access to view your ad campaigns and leads. EzyText never changes your ads or
    spends your budget.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an ad account">
    Back in EzyText, pick which **ad account** should send you leads.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review campaigns and finish">
    You'll see your campaigns (leads from all of them are captured). Click through
    to **Done**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After connecting

- New lead-form leads appear in **Contacts** within about a minute, with source
  **Google Ads**.
- Build a welcome [workflow](/automation/workflow-builder) triggered by **New
  advertising lead** to text each lead instantly.
- Track results on **Campaign Performance** and [Ad attribution](/analytics/ad-attribution).

## Account status

- **Receiving leads** — connected and active.
- **Needs attention** — the connection broke; click **Reconnect**.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a Google Ads developer token?">
    No. You just sign in with Google. The technical credentials are managed by
    EzyText.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which campaigns' leads are captured?">
    Leads from all lead forms on the connected ad account are captured — the campaign
    list in the wizard is informational.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can multiple teammates manage the connection?">
    Yes — ad connections are shared across your workspace for anyone with advertising
    permissions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Permission wasn't granted'">
    You declined a permission during Google sign-in. Try again and allow access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'The connection link expired'">
    Restart the connect flow from **Advertising → Google Ads**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'No ad accounts found'">
    The Google login has no ad accounts, or you signed in with the wrong Google
    account. Reconnect with the account that owns your ads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Account shows 'Needs attention'">
    Access expired or was revoked. Click **Reconnect** to restore lead flow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
