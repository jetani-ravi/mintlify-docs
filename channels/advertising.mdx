## Overview

The advertising channel connects your ad accounts so new **lead-form leads** flow
straight into Autocampaign AI as contacts — ready to text — and so you can see which
campaigns turn into customers. Autocampaign AI supports **Google Ads** and **Meta Ads**
(Facebook/Instagram). Find it under **Advertising** in the sidebar.

## What you get

- **Automatic lead capture** — every new lead-ad submission becomes a contact
  (source **Google Ads** or **Meta Ads**), matched by phone if they already exist.
- **Attribution** — each contact records the platform, campaign, and lead form they
  came from. See [Ad attribution](/analytics/ad-attribution).
- **Performance** — the **Campaign Performance** page shows how ad spend turns into
  contacts, appointments, and customers.

## Pages

| Page | What it shows |

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which platforms are supported?">
    Google Ads and Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram). LinkedIn and TikTok aren't
    available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do leads become contacts?">
    Lead-form submissions arrive via each platform's lead webhook and are created as
    contacts automatically, with the ad source recorded.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a developer token or API key?">
    No — you connect by signing in with Google or Facebook (OAuth). Autocampaign AI handles
    the technical credentials behind the scenes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
