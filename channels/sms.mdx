## Overview

The SMS channel is Autocampaign AI's two-way texting capability. You connect one SMS
provider — [Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Telnyx](/providers/telnyx) — and Autocampaign AI
handles sending, inbound replies, delivery status, and
[A2P 10DLC](/compliance/a2p-10dlc) registration.

## How the SMS channel is organized

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provider" icon="plug">
    Your connected Twilio or Telnyx account. Only one is active per workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Messaging service / profile" icon="gear">
    A container that groups numbers and holds the webhook + 10DLC campaign link.
    Twilio calls it a **messaging service**; Telnyx calls it a **messaging profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phone numbers" icon="hashtag">
    The numbers you send from, attached to a messaging service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="10DLC brand & campaign" icon="shield-check">
    Carrier registration that lets you send to US numbers reliably.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set up the SMS channel

1. **Connect a provider** — see [Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Telnyx](/providers/telnyx).
2. **Register your brand** — [A2P 10DLC](/compliance/a2p-10dlc).
3. **Create a messaging service / profile** and attach phone numbers.
4. **Register a campaign** and start sending.

The **Set Up Your Channel** wizard guides you through these in order right after
you connect.

## Numbers and messaging services

On the SMS channel's **Numbers** tab you can:

- **Buy a number** — filter by country, area code, or a contains-pattern, choose
  SMS/MMS/Voice capabilities, and purchase.
- **Assign a number** to a messaging service via the inline dropdown.
- **Create a messaging service** with a friendly name.

## Inbound, delivery, and compliance

- **Replies** arrive in your [Inbox](/inbox/overview), linked to the contact.
- **Delivery status** updates automatically via provider webhooks (configured for
  you — no manual setup).
- **Opt-outs** (STOP) and **opt-ins** (START) are detected on every inbound message
  — see [Opt-out](/compliance/opt-out) and [Opt-in](/compliance/opt-in).

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which provider should I choose?">
    Both are carrier-grade. Choose based on your existing account, pricing, and
    number availability. Twilio uses Account SID + Auth Token; Telnyx uses an API
    Key with Messaging scope.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I send MMS?">
    Yes, if your number and provider support it — enable the **MMS** capability when
    buying a number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I configure webhooks myself?">
    No. Autocampaign AI registers inbound and delivery-status webhooks on your messaging
    service automatically when you finish setup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Messages fail to send">
    Confirm the channel badge is **Active**, the sending number is attached to a
    messaging service, and — for US traffic — your 10DLC campaign is approved.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Replies aren't showing in the inbox">
    Finish the setup wizard so webhooks are registered, and confirm the number
    belongs to a managed messaging service.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
