## Overview

EzyText's email channel lets you send email [campaigns](/campaigns/overview) from
your own domain. Delivery is handled for you — you don't connect Gmail, Outlook, or
an SMTP server. Instead, you **verify a sending domain** and add **sender
identities**, then send.

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Intermediate (DNS setup) &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Time:** ~10 minutes plus
  DNS propagation
</Info>

## Set up email in two steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify a sending domain" icon="globe">
    Add your domain and publish the DNS records EzyText gives you so your email is
    authenticated (SPF/DKIM) and links are tracked. See
    [Sending domains](/providers/email-domains).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a sender" icon="user">
    Create a from-name and from-address on your verified domain. See
    [Senders](/providers/email-senders).
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once a sender is verified, choose it when creating an [email campaign](/campaigns/overview).

<Note>
  There's no Gmail / Microsoft 365 / SMTP connection to configure. Email sending is
  managed by EzyText — your only setup is domain verification and senders.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect my Gmail or Outlook mailbox?">
    No. EzyText sends email on your behalf from your **verified domain** — there's no
    per-mailbox OAuth or SMTP connection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need my own SendGrid or SES account?">
    No. Email delivery is handled by the platform. You only verify a domain and add
    senders.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why verify a domain at all?">
    Domain authentication (SPF/DKIM) is what makes your email land in inboxes instead
    of spam, and lets you send from `you@yourdomain.com` rather than a shared address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
