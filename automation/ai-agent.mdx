## Overview

An AI agent replies to inbound conversations in your [Inbox](/inbox/overview) using
instructions you write and knowledge you provide. It answers questions, follows
your persona and tone, cites your [knowledge base](/automation/knowledge-base), and
hands the conversation to a human when it should. Find agents under **AI Agents**
(`/ai-agents`).

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Intermediate &nbsp;·&nbsp; Agents are tuned for the **SMS inbox**.
</Info>

<Note>
  An agent only acts on conversations it's **explicitly assigned to** — it isn't a
  blanket auto-responder. You (or a workflow) assign it per conversation from the
  inbox.
</Note>

## Build an agent

The builder is a five-step wizard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identity & persona" icon="id-badge">
    Set the **Name**, an optional **Welcome message** (sent once at the start of a
    conversation), the **System prompt** (your instructions), the **Default
    language**, a **Primary purpose** (Customer Support, Sales & Lead Qualification,
    Appointment Booking, General Assistant, or Custom), and a **Tone of voice**.
    Purpose and tone shape how the agent writes; they don't add new abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model & generation" icon="microchip">
    Choose a **provider and model** (see below), and optionally a **temperature**
    and **max output tokens**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Knowledge bases" icon="book">
    Attach [knowledge bases](/automation/knowledge-base) the agent can search, and
    optionally enable **source citations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guardrails" icon="shield">
    Set an optional **reply character cap**, a **context token cap**, and **refusal
    topics** the agent should politely decline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tools & handover" icon="hand">
    Enable **knowledge retrieval**, allow **human handover**, and add **handover
    phrases** and a courtesy message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the agent can do

The agent has exactly two built-in abilities:

1. **Search your knowledge base** — retrieve relevant passages from its attached
   knowledge bases and answer from them (with optional `[n]` citations). It won't
   guess when it finds nothing.
2. **Hand over to a human** — escalate the conversation when it's out of scope, the
   contact asks for a person, or a handover phrase is detected.

<Warning>
  The Sales and Appointment-Booking purposes shape the agent's wording only. The
  agent does **not** book calendar meetings, run structured lead scoring, trigger
  workflows, or call external tools — those are prompt-driven conversation, not
  actions.
</Warning>

## How it works in the inbox

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assign the agent to a conversation">
    In the inbox, open a conversation and use the **AI assistant** card to **Assign
    agent**. This puts the conversation in **AI active** state.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The agent replies automatically">
    When a new inbound message arrives, the agent (if active and the contact isn't
    opted out) generates and sends a reply on the same channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Humans can take over anytime">
    Use **Take over** to pause the AI (**Human controlled**), or **Resume AI** to
    hand it back. Sending a manual reply also pauses the AI automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Conversation AI states:** AI active · Handover pending · Human controlled · AI
suspended. After several consecutive errors, a conversation auto-suspends so a
human can step in.

## Test before you publish

Use **Test chat** in the builder (edit mode) to chat with the agent against its
current configuration. Test chats are a dry run — they never change a conversation
or send a message.

## Activate

New agents start as **Draft**. Activate an agent to make it available for
assignment; disable it to stop it. (The list calls this "Activate"; the builder
calls it "Publish" — both do the same thing.)

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does the agent reply to everyone automatically?">
    No. It only replies on conversations you've assigned it to. Assign from the inbox
    AI assistant card, or via a workflow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can the agent book meetings or update my CRM?">
    Not today. Its two abilities are knowledge-base search and human handover. It can
    discuss booking conversationally but can't create a booking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which channels does it work on?">
    It replies on the channel the inbound message came in on and is tuned for the SMS
    inbox.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does it avoid making things up?">
    With citations enabled and knowledge retrieval on, it answers from your knowledge
    base and says it doesn't know rather than guessing. Refusal topics and guardrails
    further constrain it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The agent isn't replying">
    Check that the agent is **Active**, the conversation is **AI active** (assigned),
    and the contact isn't opted out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The conversation says 'AI suspended'">
    The agent hit several consecutive errors. Review the conversation, resolve the
    issue, and **Resume AI**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Answers ignore my documents">
    Make sure a knowledge base is attached (Step 3) and **Enable knowledge
    retrieval** is on (Step 5), and that the knowledge base finished ingesting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
