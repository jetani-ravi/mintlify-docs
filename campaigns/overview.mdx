## Overview

A campaign sends one message to many contacts. EzyText supports **SMS** and
**email** campaigns, sent immediately, scheduled for later, or on a recurring
schedule. Find them under **Campaigns** in the sidebar (`/campaigns`).

<Note>
  Before sending SMS, connect a [provider](/channels/sms) and — for US traffic —
  complete [10DLC registration](/compliance/a2p-10dlc).
</Note>

## The campaigns list

Filter by status using the tabs (each shows a live count): **All**, **Scheduled**,
**Running**, **Paused**, **Completed**, **Failed**, **Cancelled**. Columns include
Campaign, Status, Segment, Delivery progress, Date, and Cost.

Row actions depend on status:

- **Pause** — while a campaign is Running.
- **Resume** — while a campaign is Paused.
- **Cancel** — before a campaign finishes (queued messages are cancelled; already-sent stay sent).
- **Delete** — once a campaign is Completed, Failed, or Cancelled (soft delete).

<Note>
  Campaign statuses are **Scheduled, Active (shown as "Running"), Paused,
  Completed, Failed, Cancelled**. There's no draft state — creating a campaign
  sends or schedules it.
</Note>

## Create a campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a channel" icon="layer-group">
    Pick **Text message** (SMS) or **Email**. You can change it later with **Change
    channel** (which clears channel-specific fields).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sender" icon="hashtag">
    **SMS:** select one or more phone numbers — EzyText rotates them round-robin
    across recipients. **Email:** select one or more verified senders. Give the
    campaign a **name** in the summary panel (defaults to "Untitled Campaign").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Message" icon="pen">
    Compose your message. Insert **variables** (merge tags) from the toolbar, watch
    the **character counter** and SMS segment count, and toggle **link tracking** on
    detected URLs. The summary panel shows an **estimated cost**.

    For email, you also set a **Subject**, optional **Preview text**, rich content,
    and can **send a test email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audience" icon="filter">
    Select the **segment** to send to. The recipient count updates as you choose.
    See [Segments](/contacts/segments).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule" icon="clock">
    Choose **Send Now**, **Schedule for Later** (with date, time, and timezone), or
    **Recurring** (SMS only — daily/weekly/monthly with an end date or max runs).
  </Step>
</Steps>

Click **Send Campaign** in the summary panel when the status badge reads **Ready**.

<Note>
  Audience is **one segment per campaign** today (multi-segment is on the roadmap),
  and there's no segment-level exclude. Use dynamic-segment rules to shape your
  audience — see [Segments](/contacts/segments).
</Note>

### Merge tags

Personalize messages with these variables, written as `{{tag}}`:

`first_name`, `last_name`, `full_name`, `phone`, `email`, `street_address`,
`address_line_2`, `city`, `state`, `postal_code`, `country`, `full_address`

You can add a fallback: `{{first_name|"there"}}` sends "there" when the field is empty.

<Warning>
  [Custom fields](/contacts/custom-fields) are **not** available as merge tags —
  only the standard fields above.
</Warning>

## Campaign analytics

Open a campaign to see its **Overview** and **Executions** tabs.

**Summary cards:** Queued, Sent (% of recipients), Delivered (% of sent), Failed,
Cancelled, and Total cost. Email campaigns also show opens, clicks, bounces,
unsubscribes, and spam reports; SMS campaigns show link-click analytics.

The **Executions** tab (for scheduled/recurring runs) shows per-run metrics and a
**Messages** table with each recipient's From/To number, status, links opened, and
sent time.

Per-recipient message statuses: **Queued, Processing, Sent, Delivered,
Undelivered, Failed, Cancelled, Rejected**.

<Note>
  Reply tracking lives in the [Inbox](/inbox/overview), not on the campaign
  dashboard — the campaign view focuses on delivery and cost.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I send to more than one segment?">
    Not yet — one segment per campaign. Build a dynamic segment whose rules capture
    everyone you want to reach.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I duplicate or re-run a campaign?">
    Duplicate/repeat isn't available as a one-click action today. Recurring campaigns
    (SMS) cover repeated sending on a schedule.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is cost estimated?">
    SMS cost is estimated per segment (a 160-character message is one segment). The
    summary panel shows the estimate before you send.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see a reply rate?">
    Replies are handled in the unified [Inbox](/inbox/overview). The campaign
    dashboard reports delivery, cost, and (for email) engagement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Select at least one phone number' / no numbers available">
    You need an **Active**, SMS-capable number. Add or activate one in
    [Settings → Channels](/channels/sms).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recipients count is 0">
    The chosen segment is empty. Pick a different segment or add contacts to it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SMS not delivered to US numbers">
    Confirm your [10DLC campaign](/compliance/a2p-10dlc) is approved and the sending
    number is attached to the registered messaging service.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
