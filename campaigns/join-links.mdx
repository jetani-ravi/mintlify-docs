## Overview

Join links let people opt in to your messages. There are two kinds, both created on
the **Links** page (`/links`):

- **Text link** — people text a keyword (your message) to your number to join.
- **Web form** — a hosted public form people fill in to join.

Both can attach a [segment](/contacts/segments) and [tags](/contacts/tags), collect
consent, and trigger a welcome message. Each gets a shareable URL and a QR code.

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner (text link) / Intermediate (web form)
</Info>

## Text link (text-to-join)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a text link">
    On the **Links → Text Link** tab, click **Create Text Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Setup">
    Set the **Link Name** and **Custom Slug** (auto-suggested), pick the **Sender
    Phone Number**, and choose an **SMS Template** (a text-link template — the
    template body is the "keyword" people send). You can create a template inline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audience">
    Optionally attach a **Segment** and **Tags**, and set an **Auto-Reply** (sent
    immediately) and a **Welcome Message** (sent after).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & save">
    Review the SMS preview and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The public page (`/join/<slug>`) opens the visitor's Messages app pre-filled with
your number and the keyword text, plus a QR that does the same. When they send it,
EzyText creates the contact (source **Text Link**), applies your segment and tags,
records the reply as consent, and sends your welcome message.

## Web form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a web form">
    On the **Links → Web Form** tab, click **Create Web Form**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic info">
    Set the **Link Name**, **Custom Slug**, description, and active status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Form builder">
    **First Name** and **Phone** are always required. Add **optional fields** (last
    name, email, address parts). Toggle **Require consent checkbox** and set its
    label (default "I agree to receive SMS messages"). Optionally turn on **OTP
    verification** to confirm the phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post-submit">
    Attach **Tags**, set a **Thank You Message** or a **Redirect URL** (a redirect
    overrides the thank-you), add a **Welcome Message**, and pick **brand colors**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & save">
    Review the live form preview and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When someone submits, EzyText validates consent (and OTP if enabled), creates or
updates the contact (source **Web Form**), adds them to your segment, applies tags,
records a **consent snapshot** (the exact consent text, a hashed IP, user agent, and
timestamp), and sends the welcome message. They then see your thank-you screen or
are redirected.

## QR codes

Every join link can generate a **QR code** (from the list row or the detail **QR
Code** tab). Download it as a PNG and track **Total Scans** and **Unique Scans**.
For text links the QR opens Messages pre-filled; for web forms it opens the form.

## Opt-out protection

EzyText won't re-onboard an opted-out contact through a join link. If someone who
previously opted out tries to submit (or request an OTP), the request is refused —
protecting you under TCPA/GDPR. See [Opt-out management](/compliance/opt-out).

## Analytics

Each join link's detail page shows submissions over time, a submissions table
(contact name, phone, email), views, and QR scan stats. The list page summarizes
Total Links, Total Submissions, and Active Links.

## Statuses

Join links are **Active**, **Inactive**, or **Archived**. Deactivating rejects new
submissions until you reactivate; archiving (delete) makes the public URL return a
404 but keeps existing submissions and contacts.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the 'keyword' for a text link?">
    It's the SMS template body people send. EzyText matches the inbound message
    against it (exact or contains), so extra typed text still counts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do web forms verify phone numbers?">
    Optionally — turn on **OTP verification** in the form builder to require a
    6-digit code before submission.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is consent recorded?">
    Yes. When consent is required, EzyText stores the exact consent wording shown,
    plus a hashed IP, user agent, and timestamp, per submission.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I redirect after submit?">
    Yes — set a **Redirect URL** in the web-form post-submit step. It overrides the
    thank-you message.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Someone can't submit the form">
    Check the link is **Active**, they passed the **consent** checkbox (if required),
    and OTP (if enabled). Opted-out contacts are intentionally blocked.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'You're already on the list!'">
    That contact already submitted this link — duplicates per link are prevented and
    show the already-joined screen.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No welcome message arrived">
    Welcome sends are best-effort and skip opted-out contacts. Confirm the contact
    isn't opted out and that your SMS channel is connected.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
