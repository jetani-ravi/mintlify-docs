## Overview

Your **workspace** (organization) holds everything — contacts, campaigns,
channels, and team. When you sign up, a personal workspace is created for you. Set
its details at **Settings → Organization** (`/settings/organization`).

## Configure your organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open organization settings">
    Go to **Settings → Organization** and click **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set identity">
    Enter your **Organization Name** and optional **Website** and **Logo**. Typing
    your business name can auto-fill your address, phone, timezone, and currency from
    a business lookup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add contact and location">
    Fill in your **Email**, **Phone**, and business **Address**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set preferences">
    Choose your **Timezone** (defaults to UTC) and **Currency** (defaults to USD).
    These affect scheduling and cost display across the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. You can edit these anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multiple workspaces

You can create and belong to more than one organization. Use the **workspace
switcher** at the top of the sidebar to move between them, or create a new one from
there. Each workspace has its own contacts, channels, billing, and team.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between a workspace and an organization?">
    They're the same thing — your tenant in Autocampaign AI. "Workspace" is the friendly
    name; settings live under **Organization**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I rebrand the app with my logo?">
    You can set your organization **logo**. Deeper white-label branding isn't
    available today.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does timezone matter?">
    It's used for scheduling campaigns, polls, and bookings, and for displaying times
    consistently across your team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
