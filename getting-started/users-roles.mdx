## Overview

Roles determine what each member can access and do. EzyText ships with five
built-in roles and lets you create custom ones. Manage roles at **Settings →
Roles** (`/settings/roles`) and assign them on **Settings → Users**.

## Built-in roles

| Role | Typical use |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full control, including billing and ownership. One per workspace. |
| **Admin** | Everything except owner-only actions. |
| **Manager** | Runs campaigns, contacts, and inbox; limited settings. |
| **Agent** | Handles conversations and day-to-day work. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access. |

Built-in roles can't be edited or deleted.

## Assign a role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Users → Members">
    Go to **Settings → Users** and find the member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change role">
    Click **Change role**, pick a role, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The **Owner** role can't be assigned to others through the UI.
</Note>

## Create a custom role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Roles">
    Go to **Settings → Roles** and click **Create**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name it and set permissions">
    Give the role a **name** and description, then use the **permission matrix** to
    grant create/read/update/delete on each area (contacts, campaigns, inbox,
    channels, billing, and more).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and assign">
    Save the role, then assign it to members from **Users**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permissions

Permissions are grouped by area (for example, Contacts, Campaigns, Segments, Inbox,
Channels, Billing, Analytics, API keys, Webhooks). Most support the standard
create / read / update / delete actions, plus a few specialized ones (like send or
pause a campaign, reply or assign in the inbox). The UI you see adapts to your
permissions — actions you can't perform are hidden or disabled.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit a built-in role?">
    No. Built-in roles are fixed. Create a custom role if you need different
    permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I delete a role that has members?">
    Not until you reassign those members to another role.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I give someone billing access without full admin?">
    Create a custom role granting billing read/manage and the other areas you want,
    then assign it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
