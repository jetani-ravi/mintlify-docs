## Overview

The calendar gives you a single view of your bookings and external busy times. Open
it from **Calendar** in the sidebar (`/calendar`).

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner
</Info>

## Views and navigation

Switch between **Day**, **Week**, **Month**, and **Agenda** views, jump to
**Today**, and page forward/back. Times display in your workspace timezone.

The calendar shows two kinds of events:

- **Bookings** — colored by their [meeting type](/scheduling/meeting-types), titled
  with the invitee's name.
- **Busy blocks** — gray, read-only times synced from your connected external
  calendars.

## Work from the calendar

- **Filter** by meeting type, host, and status.
- **Click** a booking to open its details (reschedule, cancel, mark completed/no-show).
- **Drag** a booking to a new time to reschedule (with a confirmation). External
  busy blocks and finished bookings are read-only.

A side panel shows a mini month view and your upcoming bookings.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why do I see gray 'Busy' blocks?">
    Those come from external calendars you've connected and marked "Check for
    conflicts". They prevent double-booking. See
    [Calendar integrations](/scheduling/calendar-integrations).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create an event directly on the calendar?">
    The calendar shows bookings and busy times. New meetings are created when someone
    books a [meeting type](/scheduling/meeting-types).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
