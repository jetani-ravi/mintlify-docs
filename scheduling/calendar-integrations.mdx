## Overview

Connect an external calendar so EzyText can **block your busy times** (preventing
double-booking) and **add new bookings** to your calendar automatically. Manage
connections at **Settings → Calendars** (`/settings/calendars`).

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner &nbsp;·&nbsp; Supports **Google Calendar** and
  **Microsoft Outlook**.
</Info>

## Connect a calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Calendars">
    Click **Connect Google Calendar** or **Connect Microsoft Outlook**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in and grant access">
    You'll be sent to Google or Microsoft to authorize access, then returned to
    EzyText.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how each calendar is used">
    For each calendar on the account, toggle:

    - **Check for conflicts** — pulls busy times into your availability so booked
      slots disappear.
    - **Add bookings here** — makes this the calendar where new booking events (and
      Google Meet links) are created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What syncs

- **Busy times** from calendars marked "Check for conflicts" remove matching slots
  from your booking pages.
- **Bookings** are written to the calendar marked "Add bookings here", and updated
  or removed when a booking is rescheduled or cancelled.

<Note>
  **Google Meet** links are generated only on Google connections. Microsoft
  connections can't create Meet links.
</Note>

## Connection status

- **Connected** — syncing normally.
- **Needs attention** — access broke; click **Reconnect**.
- **Disconnected** — removed.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which providers are supported?">
    Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook (Microsoft 365).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect more than one calendar?">
    Yes — connect multiple accounts and choose per-calendar whether each blocks
    conflicts and/or receives bookings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will EzyText see my event details?">
    It uses busy/free times to block slots and writes booking events. Configure which
    calendars are used from the connection settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connection shows 'Needs attention'">
    Access expired or was revoked. Click **Reconnect** to restore syncing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Busy times aren't blocking slots">
    Make sure the calendar has **Check for conflicts** enabled, then use **Refresh
    calendars**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
