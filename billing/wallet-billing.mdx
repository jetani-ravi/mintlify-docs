## Overview

Billing in Autocampaign AI is **subscription-based**: you choose a plan that sets your
limits (entitlements), and can buy **add-ons** and **AI credits** on top. Manage it
all at **Plan & subscription** (`/billing`). Payments are handled securely by
Stripe.

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Permission:** Billing read to view,
  Billing manage to change.
</Info>

## Plans and entitlements

Your plan grants **entitlements** — the features you can use and the limits on
resources like contacts, SMS sends, segments, and seats. When you approach a limit,
Autocampaign AI warns you and points you to add-ons. Some features are gated entirely by
plan.

## AI credits

AI features consume **AI credits**, shown as an available balance. Credits come
from your plan's included grant or from buying a **credit pack** add-on. You can
enable **Auto top-up** on an add-on to automatically buy more when the balance runs
low.

<Note>
  This is not a general prepaid SMS wallet — SMS and other resources are governed by
  your plan's limits and add-on capacity, while **AI credits** are a separate,
  lot-based balance for AI features.
</Note>

## Add-ons

Buy add-ons to extend a limit (for example, more contacts or credits). Purchases
charge your saved card through Stripe; if extra verification is needed you'll be
prompted (3-D Secure).

## Invoices

Every charge produces a Stripe invoice you can view and download as a PDF from the
**Invoices** tab.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is this a prepaid wallet where I add funds for SMS?">
    Not exactly. Billing is a subscription with entitlements and add-ons. **AI
    credits** are the one wallet-like balance; SMS and other resources run on your
    plan limits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I change my payment method?">
    Open **Manage plan** to reach the Stripe billing portal, where you can update
    your card and see invoices.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I hit a limit?">
    You'll see a near-limit warning. Buy an add-on or upgrade your plan to extend the
    limit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see spend by campaign or country?">
    Usage is tracked as consumed units per resource (SMS, email, credits, etc.), not
    as per-campaign currency spend.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A purchase needs verification">
    Your bank requested 3-D Secure. Complete the prompt to finish the charge.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Auto top-up isn't refilling">
    Check the add-on's Auto top-up is enabled and not paused, and that your card is
    valid in the Stripe portal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
