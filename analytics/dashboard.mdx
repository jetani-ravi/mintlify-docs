## Overview

The dashboard is your home screen (`/`) — a snapshot of what's happening across
your workspace. Use the period selector to view the last 7 or 30 days, this month,
last month, or a custom range.

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner
</Info>

## What it shows

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Key metrics" icon="chart-simple">
    Active conversations (broken down by channel), opt-out rate, reply rate, and
    AI-handled vs human-handled — each with a trend versus the previous period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign performance" icon="paper-plane">
    A chart of sent, delivered, failed, replied, and opt-out across your campaigns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recent inbox" icon="inbox">
    Your latest conversations with an inline quick reply.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Opt-out & unsubscribe trends" icon="ban">
    Opt-out trends by channel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Poll insights" icon="square-poll-vertical">
    Active poll counts and per-poll analytics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Join link performance" icon="link-simple">
    A funnel and traffic sources for your join links.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I see cost or spend?">
    Cost is on individual campaigns and under [Wallet & billing](/billing/wallet-billing).
    The dashboard focuses on engagement, not spend.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the date range?">
    Yes — use the period selector at the top for preset ranges or a custom range.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
