## Overview

The notification **bell** in the header keeps you informed in real time. It has two
tabs — **Messages** and **Alerts** — each with its own unread count.

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner
</Info>

## Messages vs alerts

- **Messages** — inbound conversations, campaign replies, and conversations assigned
  to you.
- **Alerts** — everything else: campaign status changes, CSV import results, segment
  updates, poll events, team changes, channel/provider issues, knowledge-base
  ingestion, AI handover requests, meeting bookings, calendar/ad connection issues,
  and workflow alerts.

You only see notifications for areas your [role](/getting-started/users-roles)
allows.

## Manage notifications

- Click a notification to jump to the related item.
- **Mark as read** individually, or **Mark all as read** for the active tab.
- **View all** opens the full history.

Notifications update live over a real-time connection, and important ones also show
a toast.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see certain notifications?">
    Notifications respect permissions — you only receive ones for areas your role can
    access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I get emails or texts too?">
    The bell is for in-app notifications. EzyText separately sends transactional email
    and SMS for things like invitations and password resets.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a low-balance alert?">
    Billing alerts aren't part of the notification bell today — check
    [Wallet & billing](/billing/wallet-billing) for plan and usage status.
  </Accordion>
