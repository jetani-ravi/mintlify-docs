## Overview

Every [campaign](/campaigns/overview) has its own analytics on its detail page,
with **Overview** and **Executions** tabs.

<Info>
  **Difficulty:** Beginner
</Info>

## Summary metrics

The Overview tab shows:

- **Queued**, **Sent** (% of recipients), **Delivered** (% of sent), **Failed**,
  **Cancelled**, and **Total cost**.
- For **email** campaigns: opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, and spam reports.
- For **SMS** campaigns: link-click analytics.

## Executions and messages

For scheduled and recurring campaigns, the **Executions** tab shows per-run metrics
and a **Messages** table with each recipient's From/To numbers, status, links
opened, and sent time.

Per-recipient statuses: **Queued, Processing, Sent, Delivered, Undelivered, Failed,
Cancelled, Rejected**.

<Note>
  Replies are tracked in the [Inbox](/inbox/overview), not on the campaign
  dashboard — campaign analytics focus on delivery, cost, and (for email)
  engagement.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why isn't there a reply rate on the campaign?">
    Reply handling lives in the unified [Inbox](/inbox/overview). The campaign view
    reports delivery, cost, and engagement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is cost calculated for SMS?">
    Per message segment (a 160-character SMS is one segment). The total cost card sums
    the campaign's segments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create a segment from campaign responders?">
    Not directly from a campaign today. Use tags or the
    [poll → segment](/campaigns/polls) flow to build follow-up audiences.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
