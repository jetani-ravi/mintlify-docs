This guide takes you from a brand-new account to a live SMS campaign. Along the
way you will connect a sending channel, import contacts, and send a message.

<Info>
  **Time to complete:** ~20 minutes &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Difficulty:** Beginner
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you need:

- An EzyText account. [Sign up](https://app.ezytext.ai) if you do not have one.
- A [Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Telnyx](/providers/telnyx) account with a
  phone number, **or** an email domain you can verify for the email channel.
- A short list of contacts (name and phone number) to message. A CSV works best.

<Note>
  Sending A2P SMS to US numbers requires [10DLC registration](/compliance/a2p-10dlc).
  You can complete the steps below with a trial number first, then register before
  sending at volume.
</Note>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your workspace" icon="building">
    After signing up, name your organization and set your default country and
    timezone. See [Create your workspace](/getting-started/create-workspace).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a sending channel" icon="plug">
    Go to **Settings → Channels** and connect a provider:

    - **SMS:** follow [Connect Twilio](/providers/twilio) or [Connect Telnyx](/providers/telnyx).
    - **Email:** verify a domain and sender in [Email channel](/channels/email).

    A channel shows a green **Connected** badge once credentials are valid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your contacts" icon="file-import">
    Open **Contacts → Import**, upload your CSV, and map the columns to contact
    fields. Confirm consent, then run the import. Full walkthrough:
    [Import contacts](/contacts/import-csv).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a campaign" icon="paper-plane">
    Go to **Campaigns → New**, pick your sending number, write your message
    (add merge tags like `{{first_name}}`), and choose who receives it.
    See [Campaigns](/campaigns/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or schedule" icon="clock">
    Send now or schedule for later, then watch delivery and replies come in on
    the [Dashboard](/analytics/dashboard) and in your [Inbox](/inbox/overview).
  </Step>
</Steps>
